## 2. Precision Machining Technology Details

### 2.1 Spherical Surface Machining Technology

#### 2.1.1 Traditional Turning Process

**Process Characteristics:**

- **Machining Method**: Lathe interpolation turning of ball head
- **Machining Accuracy**: Roundness 0.01–0.02 mm
- **Surface Roughness**: Ra 0.4–0.8 μm
- **Machining Efficiency**: Medium

**Process Problems:**

- **Insufficient Accuracy**: Difficult to achieve high accuracy requirements
- **Low Efficiency**: Long machining time
- **Poor Consistency**: Poor batch consistency

#### 2.1.2 Rolling Process

**Process Characteristics:**

- **Machining Method**: Roller extrusion for polishing
- **Machining Accuracy**: Roundness 0.005–0.01 mm
- **Surface Roughness**: Ra 0.1–0.2 μm
- **Machining Efficiency**: Relatively high

**Process Problems:**

- **Accuracy Wear**: Mechanical accuracy wears with long-term use
- **Mold Deformation**: Local deformation of polishing pressure roller
- **Maintenance Cost**: Frequent maintenance and mold replacement required

#### 2.1.3 Specialized Spherical Surface Processing Equipment (International Advanced Equipment)

**Process Characteristics:**

- **Machining Method**: Specialized spherical surface processing equipment completes in one step
- **Machining Accuracy**: Roundness < 0.005 mm
- **Surface Roughness**: Ra 0.02–0.1 μm
- **Machining Efficiency**: High

**Process Advantages:**

- **High Accuracy**: Can complete spherical surface processing and extrusion in one step, ensuring true roundness and surface roughness
- **Long Precision**: Precision maintained for a long time
- **Low Cost**: Low adjustment and maintenance costs
- **High Consistency**: Good batch consistency

**International Advanced Equipment:**

- **European Equipment**: High-precision spherical surface processing equipment
- **American Equipment**: High-efficiency spherical surface processing equipment
- **Japanese Equipment**: Precision spherical surface processing equipment

#### 2.1.4 Process Comparison Analysis
