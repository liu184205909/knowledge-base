## 1. Spherical Surface Processing Equipment Details

### 1.1 Equipment Types and Classification (International Equipment)

Spherical surface processing equipment is classified into different types according to processing methods and accuracy requirements:

**Classification by Processing Method:**

- **Turning Equipment**: Traditional turning processing
- **Rolling Equipment**: Rolling processing
- **Specialized Equipment**: Specialized spherical surface processing equipment (international advanced equipment)

**Classification by Accuracy Grade:**

- **Standard Accuracy Equipment**: Roundness 0.01–0.02 mm
- **High Accuracy Equipment**: Roundness 0.005–0.01 mm
- **Ultra-High Accuracy Equipment**: Roundness < 0.005 mm

**Classification by Automation Level:**

- **Manual Equipment**: Manual operation
- **Semi-Automatic Equipment**: Partial automation
- **Fully Automatic Equipment**: Full automation

**Specialized Spherical Surface Processing Equipment Structure:**

- **Spindle System**: High-precision spindle, ensures processing accuracy

- Consider equipment utilization
