Use `Earthward` as the primary wordmark. The logo should follow the same brand color system used by the Crystal Meaning article CSS.

- `Earth` text and icon outline = Deep Ink `#1A1A2E`
- `Ward` text and primary icon line = Primary Green `#2D6A4F`
- accent line = Earth Copper `#A66A43`
- background = white `#FFFFFF`

- `Earth` text and icon outline = Warm Ivory `#F7F3EA`

- accent line = muted gold `#C9A96E`

### Related Site Colors

- Deep Green `#1B4332` remains available for hover/deep accents.
- Pale Green `#F0F7F4` remains available for article callouts and light UI surfaces.
