@@@AI_CRYSTAL_LABRADORITE@@@
<p>Labradorite belongs to the plagioclase feldspar family and exhibits labradorescence, an iridescent effect created by light scattering across microscopic structural layers. While Arctic Indigenous cultures historically valued feldspars for their resilience, modern crystal traditions often link the stone to boundary awareness and intuitive reflection. When paired with Human Design concepts, the shifting play of color symbolizes the fluid nature of aura and energy exchange. Many find that wearing it encourages quiet consideration of when to initiate, when to await an invitation, and how to maintain personal space without disengaging from meaningful connection.</p>
