@@@AI_M6_DEEPEN@@@
In Five Elements theory, the five elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water) interact in generating cycles (one feeds the next) and controlling cycles (one restrains the next). Water controls Fire the way water extinguishes a flame — useful in small amounts (it keeps Fire from becoming destructive) but potentially extinguishing in large amounts (it can put the Fire out entirely). For a Fire-element Snake, this is the logic behind the guideline to use large Water-element stacks (Aquamarine, Lapis Lazuli, Sodalite in big amounts) mindfully. It doesn't mean a Snake can never wear Aquamarine; it means the tradition suggests Fire-supporting and grounding stones (Carnelian in small accents, Tiger's Eye, Smoky Quartz) as the dominant tone, with Water stones as accent pieces rather than the main stack.
