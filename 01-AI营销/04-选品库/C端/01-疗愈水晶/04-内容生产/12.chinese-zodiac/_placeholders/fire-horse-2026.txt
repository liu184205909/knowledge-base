Across Chinese zodiac tradition, ben ming nian is read as a year of change and visibility rather than pure smooth sailing; the folk idea is that you "offend Tai Sui" (犯太岁) in your own year, which is a symbolic way of saying the year tends to surface shifts and tests. The stones most commonly recommended are Black Tourmaline, Smoky Quartz, and Jade: grounding, protective, and steady.

So 2026 is Fire sitting on Fire: double Yang Fire energy, traditionally associated with boldness, speed, visibility, and amplified ambition. It's the same Horse archetype that comes every twelve years, but the Fire-Horse specifically only comes every sixty. The cultural reading is "powerful year, needs conscious balancing" — not inherently good or bad.
