About Malachite

Malachite is a vivid green, banded copper mineral, traditionally associated with transformation, protection, and personal growth. In crystal practice it is often linked with the Heart and Solar Plexus chakras, which makes it a natural match for emotional release, life transitions, and breaking old patterns. Common forms include pendants, tumbled stones, spheres, and carved pieces.

Mineralogically, Malachite is copper carbonate hydroxide (Cu₂CO₃(OH)₂) with a Mohs hardness of only 3.5–4 — much softer than Carnelian. That softness means it is better suited to pendants, palm stones, spheres, and display carvings than to rings that take daily knocks, and it should be kept out of water and salt water. Handle it gently and store it away from harder stones that may scratch its surface.
