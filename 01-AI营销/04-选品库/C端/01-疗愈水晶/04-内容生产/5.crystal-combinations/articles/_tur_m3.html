About Red Jasper

Red Jasper is a brick-red to terracotta stone, traditionally associated with grounding, strength, endurance, and courage. In crystal practice it is often linked with the Root and Sacral chakras, which makes it a natural fit for steady focus, stamina, and stress relief. Common forms include bracelets, pendants, tumbled stones, palm stones, and raw pieces.

Mineralogically, Red Jasper is silicon dioxide (SiO₂) colored by hematite (iron oxide), with a Mohs hardness of 6.5–7 — durable enough for everyday bracelets, pendants, and rings. It tolerates a brief rinse, is sun-safe, and is salt-safe, which makes it one of the easier stones to care for in a daily jewelry rotation. Where Turquoise asks for gentle, dry handling, Red Jasper is the more forgiving everyday companion.
