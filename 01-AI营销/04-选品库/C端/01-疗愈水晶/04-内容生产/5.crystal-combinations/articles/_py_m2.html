About Tiger Eye

Tiger Eye is a golden-brown variety of quartz with a silky, chatoyant sheen, traditionally associated with courage, confidence, and protection. In crystal practice it's often linked with the Solar Plexus, Root, and Sacral chakras, which makes it a natural fit for willpower, grounding, and personal-power work. Common forms include cabochons, beads, tumbled stones, and spheres — well suited to everyday wear.

Mineralogically, Tiger Eye is silicon dioxide (SiO₂) intergrown with crocidolite fibers, with a Mohs hardness of 6.5–7, durable enough for daily bracelets, pendants, and rings. Its golden chatoyancy comes from iron oxide and the included fibers. It tolerates only a brief rinse with water and should be kept away from salt water, though it's sun-safe.
