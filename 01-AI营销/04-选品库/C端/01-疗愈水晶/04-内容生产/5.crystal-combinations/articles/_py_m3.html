About Pyrite

Pyrite is a brass-yellow, metallic stone traditionally associated with abundance, confidence, and willpower. In crystal practice it's linked with the Solar Plexus chakra, which makes it a natural match for motivation, wealth mindset, and focused action. Common forms include natural cubic crystals, clusters, tumbled stones, and spheres — many people keep a Pyrite cube or cluster on a desk or altar rather than wearing it daily.

Mineralogically, Pyrite is iron sulfide (FeS₂) with a Mohs hardness of 6–6.5, durable in polished form but best kept dry because it can oxidize and tarnish with prolonged moisture. It should be kept away from water and salt, though it's sun-safe. Its bright metallic shine comes from its native iron sulfide composition.
