About Carnelian

Carnelian is a warm orange to reddish-brown variety of chalcedony, traditionally associated with courage, motivation, and creative energy. In crystal practice it is often linked with the Sacral, Solar Plexus, and Root chakras, which makes it a popular stone for confidence, starting new projects, and creative work. Common forms include bracelets, rings, pendants, and tumbled or cabochon stones.

Mineralogically, Carnelian is silicon dioxide (SiO₂) with a Mohs hardness of 7, durable enough for everyday bracelets, rings, and pendants. Its rich color comes from iron oxide inclusions. Carnelian tolerates only a brief rinse with water and should be kept away from long salt soaks, though its color stays stable in sunlight.
