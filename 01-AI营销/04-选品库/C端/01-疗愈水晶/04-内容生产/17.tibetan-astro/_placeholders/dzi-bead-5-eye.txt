Historically worn as protective amulets, this variant features five distinct circular motifs arranged along its surface. In Tibetan tradition, each eye corresponds to one of the Five Wisdom Buddhas—Vairochana, Akshobhya, Ratnasambhava, Amitabha, and Amoghasiddhi—and the bead is traditionally regarded as a symbol of comprehensive blessing intended to bring harmony across different areas of daily life.</p>
