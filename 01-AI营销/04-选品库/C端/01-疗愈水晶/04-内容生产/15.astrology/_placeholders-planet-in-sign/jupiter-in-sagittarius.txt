<p><strong>Citrine</strong> is a variety of silicon dioxide distinguished by its pale yellow to golden hues, typically forming when trace iron interacts with geothermal heat or natural radiation during crystallization. Historically, collectors associated its solar warmth with mental vitality and decision-making clarity, while modern mindfulness practices use it as a focal point for setting intentional directions. When held or worn, this stone may invite reflection on how you allocate your energy, helping to distinguish between productive expansion and scattered pursuits.</p>

@@@AI_CRYSTAL_LAPIS@@@
<p><strong>Lapis Lazuli</strong> is a metamorphic rock composed primarily of lazurite, calcite, and pyrite, yielding its deep blue coloration through sulfur-bearing minerals. Ancient artisans ground it for ultramarine pigments and carved it into administrative seals, valuing its association with clear communication and objective observation. In contemporary reflective practice, lapis is often used to quiet mental chatter and encourage honest self-assessment. Paired with the forward-looking nature of this astrological placement, it may support grounding expansive ideas into actionable, truthful steps.</p>
