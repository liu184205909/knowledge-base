@@@AI_CRYSTAL_CITRINE@@@
<p><strong>Citrine</strong> is a quartz variety whose golden-to-apricot tones originate from trace iron impurities within its sodium potassium aluminum silicate lattice, and it exhibits well-documented piezoelectric behavior when subjected to mechanical stress. Across Mediterranean and European folk traditions, miners and merchants associated the stone with sunlit abundance and mental clarity. Within the context of <strong>jupiter in leo</strong>, many use citrine as a physical reminder to channel expansive fire energy into sustained creative work, pausing to observe whether motivation arises from genuine passion or a craving for external applause.</p>

@@@AI_CRYSTAL_LAPIS@@@
<p><strong>Lapis lazuli</strong> is a metamorphic rock primarily composed of lazurite, typically intergrown with calcite veining and pyrite flecks that produce its signature deep blue and metallic gold matrix. Ancient Egyptian and Mesopotamian cultures reserved it for ceremonial objects and later ground it into ultramarine pigment, while contemporary crystal practices link it to vocal expression and inner honesty. For those exploring <strong>jupiter in leo</strong>, lapis serves as a grounding counterweight to fiery expansion, encouraging clear communication and reminding wearers to distinguish between performative confidence and quiet self-assurance.</p>
