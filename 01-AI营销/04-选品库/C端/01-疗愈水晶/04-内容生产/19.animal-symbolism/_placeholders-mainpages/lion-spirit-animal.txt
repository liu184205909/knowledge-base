@@@AI_CRYSTAL_CARNELIAN@@@
<p><strong>Carnelian</strong> is an orange-red variety of chalcedony, itself a microcrystalline form of silicon dioxide. Its warm tones derive from iron oxide impurities, and it typically ranks between six and six and a half on the Mohs hardness scale. Geologically, it crystallizes in amygdules of basaltic and other volcanic rocks, often forming in geodes alongside calcite or zeolites. Contemporary spiritual practice sometimes aligns carnelian with the <strong>lion spirit animal</strong> to highlight motivation, creative drive, and courageous action.
