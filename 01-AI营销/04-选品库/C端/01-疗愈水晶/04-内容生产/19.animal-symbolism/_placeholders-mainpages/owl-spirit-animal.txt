<p><strong>Science:</strong> Lapis lazuli is a metamorphic rock dominated by lazurite, typically intergrown with calcite and pyrite, giving it a deep blue matrix streaked with golden flecks. <strong>Tradition:</strong> Ancient Near Eastern civilizations mined it extensively, yet today it functions as a modern mindfulness tool rather than a historical protective charm. <strong>Mindfulness:</strong> When paired with the owl archetype, lapis encourages direct questioning: “What am I avoiding seeing?” Placing the stone nearby while recording dreams or reviewing daily interactions can reinforce a habit of honest observation.
