@@@AI_M3_CITRINE_THREE_ELEMENT@@@
<p>Citrine is a variety of quartz composed of silicon dioxide, with its warm yellow-orange hue derived from iron impurities and heat exposure during formation. Historically, it has been associated with warmth, sunlight, and imagery of abundance due to its sunny appearance. In mindfulness, it serves as a warm-toned tactile cue for a positive intention or resolve, helping to anchor feelings of optimism.</p>

@@@AI_M3_PYRITE_THREE_ELEMENT@@@
<p>Pyrite, chemically known as iron disulfide, features a metallic gold appearance and forms cubic crystals that can produce sparks when struck. Traditionally, it has been linked to the sun, vitality, and confident action because of its brilliant reflectivity. Mindfully, its bright surface acts as a visual cue for a confident, sun-like intention, encouraging assertive yet grounded focus.</p>

@@@AI_M3_CLEAR_QUARTZ_THREE_ELEMENT@@@
<p>Clear quartz is a crystalline form of silicon dioxide, noted for its transparency and piezoelectric properties used in electronic timing devices. It is widely described across cultures as a 'master' stone suitable for placement anywhere on the body during practice. As a mindfulness tool, it provides a neutral, colorless focal point that does not compete with visualization imagery, allowing for pure mental clarity.</p>

@@@AI_M3_CARNELIAN_THREE_ELEMENT@@@
<p>Carnelian is a chalcedony variety of silicon dioxide, displaying a translucent orange-red color caused by iron oxide inclusions. Ancient cultures in Egypt and Rome often associated it with vitality and courageous action due to its fiery hue. In a meditative context, its warm translucent tone serves as a cue for gentle activation rather than agitation, supporting sustained energy without overwhelm.</p>

@@@AI_M4_STEP_3_DEEPEN@@@
