@@@AI_GIFT_1_MEANING@@@
<p><strong>Citrine</strong> is a translucent yellow quartz composed primarily of silicon dioxide. Historically called the merchant’s stone, it has long been associated with attracting new opportunities and sustaining positive energy in commercial spaces.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_1_WHY@@@
<p>This stone suits recipients navigating career shifts or launching startups because its bright hue mirrors optimism and active pursuit of goals. Gifting citrine acknowledges their drive while providing a daily touchstone for confidence.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_1_HOWTO@@@
<p>Present the piece inside a small wooden box alongside a handwritten note about a specific professional milestone you admire. Encourage them to keep it near their workspace or wear it as a pendant during important meetings.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_2_MEANING@@@
<p><strong>Pyrite</strong> forms from iron and sulfur, crystallizing into striking metallic cubes that catch light with a sharp brilliance. Many traditions view its unyielding structure as a symbol of grounded value and steadfast determination.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_2_WHY@@@
<p>It fits recipients who handle finances or lead teams, as the stone’s resilient nature reflects practical strategy over quick wins. The gift signals respect for their discipline and long-term vision.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_2_HOWTO@@@
<p>Pair a pyrite bracelet with a leather cord to emphasize its rugged elegance. Include a card suggesting they place a raw specimen on their desk to anchor daily planning sessions.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_3_MEANING@@@
<p><strong>Green Aventurine</strong> is a feldspathic quartz containing tiny mica flakes that create a subtle shimmer known as aventurescence. Folklore frequently links its soft green tones to gentle luck and unexpected opportunities.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_3_WHY@@@
<p>This stone appeals to creative professionals or students entering competitive fields, where open doors often come from preparation meeting chance. Offering aventurine recognizes their patience and openness to growth.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_3_HOWTO@@@
<p>Wrap the jewelry in tissue paper stamped with a simple leaf motif to echo its natural origins. Suggest wearing it during portfolio reviews or grant applications as a quiet confidence boost.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_4_MEANING@@@
<p><strong>Green Jade</strong> comprises dense silicate minerals, typically nephrite or jadeite, prized for their exceptional toughness and smooth polish. In East Asian culture, it represents moral clarity, wisdom, and enduring success.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_4_WHY@@@
<p>Its historical weight makes it appropriate for recipients celebrating anniversaries, promotions, or generational milestones. The gift conveys respect for their established path and steady character.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_4_HOWTO@@@
<p>Present a jade bangle or pendant in a silk pouch, accompanied by a brief note on the stone’s cultural significance. Recommend keeping it close to the heart during significant life transitions.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_5_MEANING@@@
<p><strong>Tiger’s Eye</strong> develops when crocidolite fibers are replaced by silica during metamorphism, yielding a silky banding effect. Traditional lore associates its golden-brown stripes with clarity, courage, and focused intent.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_5_WHY@@@
<p>This piece suits leaders or freelancers managing multiple priorities, as the stone’s grounding visual rhythm encourages disciplined execution. It serves as a wearable reminder to stay aligned with core objectives.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_5_HOWTO@@@
<p>Gift a tiger’s eye ring or cufflinks with a matte black presentation case to highlight its natural luster. Suggest using it as a tactile focus tool during high-stakes negotiations or project launches.</p>
