@@@AI_GIFT_2_MEANING@@@
<p>Belonging to the orthoclase feldspar group, moonstone displays monoclinic symmetry and exhibits adularescence—a glowing surface sheen caused by light scattering between alternating layers of two feldspar minerals. In various cultural contexts, this optical effect has long been associated with cyclical change and inner clarity. Wearing it serves as a subtle anchor during periods of transition.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_2_WHY@@@
<p>Ideal for birthdays marking a new chapter, career shifts, or springtime renewals, the stone’s shifting luster mirrors the unpredictability of fresh starts. It pairs well with silver or white gold settings that emphasize its ethereal quality.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_2_HOWTO@@@

@@@AI_GIFT_3_WHY@@@

@@@AI_GIFT_4_WHY@@@

@@@AI_GIFT_5_MEANING@@@

Its lower hardness rating calls for mindful placement in jewelry design.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_5_WHY@@@
