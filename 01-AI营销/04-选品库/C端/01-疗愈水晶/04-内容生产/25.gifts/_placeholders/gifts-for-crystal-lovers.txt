@@@AI_GIFT_2_MEANING@@@

@@@AI_GIFT_2_WHY@@@
<p>Crystal enthusiasts often seek selenite not just for adornment but for its practical role in maintaining clean energy around other stones. Geologists value it for its fluorescence under ultraviolet light, which reveals internal structural patterns invisible to the naked eye.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_4_WHY@@@
