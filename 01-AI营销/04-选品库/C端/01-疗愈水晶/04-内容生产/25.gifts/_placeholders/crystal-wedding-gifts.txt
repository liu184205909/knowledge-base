@@@AI_GIFT_2_MEANING@@@
<p>Moonstone forms in the orthorhombic system and displays its signature glow through light scattering between alternating microscopic layers of orthoclase and albite feldspar. Across Indian and Vedic practices, this optical effect traditionally represents lunar cycles and emotional fluidity, qualities often celebrated in union. The layered structure itself serves as a natural metaphor for two distinct paths gradually aligning.</p>

@@@AI_GIFT_2_WHY@@@

@@@AI_GIFT_2_HOWTO@@@

@@@AI_GIFT_3_WHY@@@
