About Our Tarot Readings







Tarotoo is an innovative, interactive platform offering free online tarot readings. We combine the traditional meanings and symbolism of the Rider-Waite tarot deck with advanced AI technology to create a new, engaging format of tarot reading, designed for spiritual growth, self-reflection, and a better understanding of your emotions, choices, and patterns.





Reviewed by a professional tarot reader. The tarot reading process on our website, AI logic, prompts, and symbolic framework have been developed in collaboration with and reviewed by Inbaal Honigman, a professional tarot reader, psychic, and astrologer with over 25 years of experience.








With animated tarot cards. Each tarot reading provides unique, in-depth interpretations and is accompanied by atmospheric animated tarot cards that bring the archetypes to life. The cards respect traditional Rider-Waite symbolism while adding visual depth and emotional resonance.





Personalized readings. Unlike static tarot explanations from traditional sites, every reading is shaped by your input. The result is not a generic explanation, the system interprets card combinations in the context of your question, your focus, and the cards you choose.








Multiple spreads. Tarotoo supports multiple spreads, including Love, Yes/No, Daily, and general 1-, 3-, and 5-card readings. The platform is available in multiple languages and continues to evolve with improvements to its reading logic, user experience, and interpretation quality.





Note: Tarotoo does not predict the future and is not a substitute for medical, legal, financial, or other professional advice.

Center Yourself Before Picking the Cards

Getting a Tarot reading relies on the energy you have within you. Your energy connects to the cards, allowing your intuition to guide you regarding what cards to pick.

This means that it is really important to center your energy before picking the cards. Don’t rush into the reading. You should also avoid performing this free tarot card reading if your emotions are heightened. Anger, sadness, or other negative emotions will affect your understanding of the cards.

Before you pull tarot cards, take a moment for meditation. Inhale through your nose and out through your mouth, visualizing a flow of energy through you. Imagine any negative energy being released through your exhaling.

Center your energy by focusing on the present. Go through your senses one by one. What can you feel? Can you hear anything? How about your smells?

Doing this allows you to focus your energy on the Tarot reading.

Reflect On The Meanings As You Pick The Cards

When you feel ready, it is time to pick the tarot cards. You will be selecting five different cards, one for each position. Now, it is important not to click and pick randomly without reflecting on the position you are picking the card for.

Before selecting each card in our free online tarot reading, remind yourself of what you are picking the card for. You may wish to say it out loud. For example, when you are picking the card that reveals your strengths, you can say, ‘this card will show me my strengths.’

Doing this will allow your intuition to guide you, meaning that you will pick the right card for the position.


Reflect on the Cards - Tap Into Your Intuition!

Once you have the five cards, it is time to reflect on their meanings. Some cards are pretty obvious, and you will know straight away what they are referring to. But, you may need to use your intuition when reflecting on certain cards and their positions.

When you are faced with a card that is difficult to understand, there are two main aspects of the card to go through. These are keywords and symbolism. Every card has keywords that represent their general meaning. Take a look at those keywords and reflect on what they might mean for the position the card is in. You may also wish to reflect on your own associations with these keywords. How do they make you feel? How do you see them in your general life? Use your gut to guide you. If you feel that this card represents something specific in your life, it probably does.

The next way you can use your intuition when reading the cards is by reflecting on the imagery and symbols on the card that has been revealed. Symbolism is extremely important in Tarot cards, so in this free online tarot reading too, and we can gain a lot of understanding by examining the imagery of the cards. What sticks out to you? How does the imagery of the card make you feel?


Stick With The Cards You Get

Sometimes, a Tarot reading will tell us something we don’t want to hear. Or, it may give us an answer we just can’t figure out.

You may want to try again and pick five new cards, but you need to stick with the cards you get. Repeating the tarot cards reading until you get the answers you want is not helpful at all. You received these five specific cards for a reason!

You may find it difficult to understand specific cards and how they relate to their certain position. This doesn’t mean that they are wrong! If you have a journal, note down the cards you received. Over time, things may become clearer to understand.


How Can You Implement What You Have Learned in Your Life?

Finally, take your time to think about how you can implement what you have learned in your life. By reflecting on the cards and what they mean, you are able to move forward on your true path in life.

We recommend repeating our general 5-card Tarot reading every couple of months and journaling the cards. This allows you to track your progress in life and allow the Tarot to guide you forward. You can use our 3-card spread any time you have a question. Please remember that tarot readings are intended for insight and reflection only.

Don’t Rush Into It

Before selecting your cards, take a moment to relax. Clear your mind and acknowledge your current emotions. Let go of any negative thoughts and concentrate on the here and now. This helps you connect better with the cards and get a more precise reading about your past, present, and future.

Trust Yourself

When you are picking the cards for your Past, Present, Future reading trust yourself and your gut feeling. You'll feel drawn to certain cards, and there’s a reason for that! Don’t overthink it; let your inner voice guide you. Pick the cards slowly and think about their meaning for each position. You might find it helpful to speak out loud as you choose your cards.

Go Back To Your Cards

Once you have received your three cards, we suggest writing them down in a Tarot journal. This approach helps you connect with the cards, bringing their energy into your life more deeply.

Write down the Past, Present, and Future cards you drew and note your thoughts about them. Do they make sense to you? Do any of the cards seem confusing? It's completely normal if some cards don't make total sense right away! It doesn't mean you picked the wrong cards; it just takes time to understand their meanings for you.

At the end of the day, revisit your Tarot journal and think about how the cards have influenced your day. Some cards may become clearer, and you might want to add more reflections to your journal.

Make Tarot Reading a Habit

This free online 3 card tarot reading focuses on your past, present, and future. It's a simple and popular way to understand your life better.

You can do this reading any time, not just every day. By looking at your past, present, and future through tarot cards, you can see patterns and insights about your life. This helps you grow and improve in many ways.

Make this tarot reading a regular part of your routine to gain deeper knowledge about yourself and your path in life.
