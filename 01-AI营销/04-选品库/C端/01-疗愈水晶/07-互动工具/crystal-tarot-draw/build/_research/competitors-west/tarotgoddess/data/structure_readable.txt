can tarot predict the future?

When it comes to the tarot everyone wants to know "what's going to happen?" While a tarot or oracle readings can't guarantee the future, it can help you figure out your best way forward ... and if a prediction turns out to be on the mark, all the better. Bottom line: we consider a tarot reading a reflection of the moment, representing a possible outcome if things continue without change—the future is never written in stone.
