Frequently asked questions
How does a free online tarot reading work?
You select a spread (such as Celtic Cross or a 3-card spread), then choose cards from our virtual 78-card deck. Each card you pick is interpreted based on its position and meaning. Your reading is personalized to the exact cards and order you select—no two readings are alike.
What is the Celtic Cross tarot spread?
The Celtic Cross is a 10-card spread and one of the most popular tarot layouts. It explores your present situation, past influences, hopes, fears, external forces, and likely outcome. It gives a comprehensive answer to any question.
What's the difference between a 3-card and 10-card tarot reading?
3-card spreads (like Past Present Future or Love) are quick and focused—ideal for specific questions. The 10-card Celtic Cross is more detailed and explores multiple facets of your situation. Choose based on how much depth you want.
How do reversed cards work?
Reversed cards (drawn upside down) have different meanings than upright cards—they often indicate blocked energy, delayed outcomes, or internal reflection. When you enable reversed cards, you choose the percentage chance each card will appear reversed. Some tarot readers believe online readings should not use reversed cards, since physical shuffling is part of the tradition. Others prefer 50% for maximum depth and the full range of reversed meanings. We recommend 5% for a subtle hint of reversal, 10% for a balanced approach, or 50% if you want to explore reversed meanings in depth. The choice is yours.
Can I use tarot for yes/no questions?
Yes. Our Yes/No spread uses a single card to give a clear yes or no answer. Phrase your question so that "yes" would be the favorable outcome for the most accurate reading.
