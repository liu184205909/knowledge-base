What Is a Chakra Test?

A chakra test is a self-reflection quiz that helps identify which of your seven energy centers may be blocked or out of balance. By answering honest questions about how you currently feel across different areas of life, the test points to where your energy may be stuck — and the crystals traditionally used to support that chakra. It is a reflective tool for awareness, not a medical or psychological diagnosis.

Symptoms of a Blocked Chakra

Each chakra shows different signs when blocked. If you've been feeling stuck in a specific area of life, these symptoms can help you identify which energy center needs attention — even before taking the test.

Root Chakra Blocked — Signs

Feeling ungrounded, anxious about money or safety, disconnected from your body, restless, or unable to settle. You may worry constantly about basic needs or feel like you don't belong anywhere. Physical signs sometimes include lower back pain, leg issues, or fatigue.

Sacral Chakra Blocked — Signs

Creative numbness, emotional suppression, low motivation for pleasure, or guilt around enjoyment. You may feel disconnected from your feelings or struggle to experience joy. Physical signs can include reproductive or lower abdominal discomfort.

Solar Plexus Chakra Blocked — Signs

Self-doubt, indecision, lack of motivation, or feeling powerless and stuck. On the opposite end, a blocked solar plexus can manifest as excessive control, anger, or aggression — energy that has nowhere constructive to go.

Heart Chakra Blocked — Signs

Difficulty giving or receiving love, holding grudges, emotional withdrawal, or feeling cold and distant. You may fear vulnerability or shut down when someone gets close. Physical signs sometimes include chest tightness or shallow breathing.

Throat Chakra Blocked — Signs

Fear of speaking up, feeling unheard or misunderstood, compulsive lying or over-talking to compensate. You may swallow your truth or struggle to express what you really feel. Physical signs can include sore throat, neck stiffness, or thyroid issues.

Third Eye Chakra Blocked — Signs

Confusion, poor focus, ignoring your intuition, inability to see a clear path forward, or feeling disconnected from your inner guidance. You may overthink everything yet feel no clarity. Physical signs sometimes include headaches or sleep issues.

Crown Chakra Blocked — Signs

Feeling spiritually disconnected, lacking purpose or meaning, emptiness, or a sense that life is mechanical. You may feel isolated from something greater than yourself. Physical signs can include brain fog or a sense of heaviness in the head.

The 7 Chakras and What They Govern

Each chakra maps to a specific life area. When balanced, energy flows freely; when blocked, you may feel stuck in that domain.

  • Root (Muladhara) — security, stability, grounding, survival instincts.
  • Sacral (Svadhisthana) — creativity, emotion, pleasure, sensuality.
  • Solar Plexus (Manipura) — confidence, willpower, self-esteem, personal power.
  • Heart (Anahata) — love, compassion, forgiveness, connection.
  • Throat (Vishuddha) — communication, truth, self-expression.
  • Third Eye (Ajna) — intuition, insight, imagination, clarity.
  • Crown (Sahasrara) — spirituality, wisdom, purpose, universal connection.

How to Balance a Blocked Chakra

Once the test reveals your most blocked chakra, the path forward blends intention with simple daily practice. Here are the most effective traditional methods:

Work with Chakra Crystals

Crystals are the most accessible tool for chakra work. Here's how to use them:

  • Choose the right stone for your blocked chakra (see the guide below).
  • Wear it as jewelry — a bracelet or pendant keeps the stone's energy close to your body throughout the day.
  • Meditate with it — place the crystal on or near the chakra's body location during quiet reflection.
  • Carry it in your pocket or bag as a tactile reminder of your intention.
  • Place it on your nightstand or desk to keep the energy in your space.

Chakra Meditation

A simple daily practice: sit quietly, close your eyes, and focus on the blocked chakra's location and color. Visualize it becoming clear, open, and vibrant. Spend 3–5 minutes per chakra, working from root to crown. Pair this with the crystal placed on the relevant body area for a deeper session.

How Accurate Is a Chakra Test?

A chakra test reflects how you feel right now, not a fixed state. Your energy shifts with stress, seasons, relationships, and intentional practice — so results can change from week to week. The most honest answers give the most useful insight. Think of the test as a mirror for where to focus your attention, paired with the crystals traditionally used to support that focus. Retake it every few weeks to track how your balance evolves.

Chakra Test FAQ

Which chakra is blocked? +

The test above tells you — it flags the energy center scoring lowest based on your honest answers. Everyone's pattern differs: one person may have a blocked root (security), another a blocked throat (expression). Take the test to find yours.

How do I know which chakra is blocked? +

Two ways: take the 14-question test above, or read the symptoms in the section above and notice which resonates — anxiety about money points to root, creative numbness to sacral, fear of speaking up to throat, and so on.

What happens when a chakra is blocked? +

A blocked chakra shows up as imbalance in its life area — a blocked heart may feel like difficulty giving or receiving love; a blocked solar plexus like low confidence; a blocked third eye like poor focus and ignoring intuition. See the Symptoms section above for each chakra's specific signs.

How long does it take to unblock a chakra? +

It varies. Some feel a shift within days of wearing the right crystal and setting an intention; deeper patterns take weeks or months. Consistency matters more than intensity — retake the test periodically to track progress.
