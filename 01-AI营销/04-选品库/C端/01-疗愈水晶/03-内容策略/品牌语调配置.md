## 1. 品牌语调

| 维度 | 要求 |
|------|------|
| 核心气质 | 温和、可信、灵性但不玄乎 |
| 表达方式 | 先给清晰结论，再解释科学/灵性/心理三个视角 |
| 用户关系 | 像懂水晶的朋友和向导，不像夸张销售员 |
| 转化方式 | 用场景、情绪和选择建议引导购买，不硬推 |
| 证据边界 | 可以讲传统寓意、用户体验和心理暗示；不宣称治疗疾病或保证结果 |

## 2. 正面语气案例

| 场景 | 可参考表达 | 为什么可用 |
|------|------------|------------|
| 水晶含义 | "Rose Quartz is known as the stone of unconditional love, often used as a gentle reminder to practice self-compassion." | 保留灵性传统，但不做绝对化承诺 |
| 产品推荐 | "If you want a bracelet for calmer evenings, Amethyst is a good starting point because its meaning, color, and ritual use all point toward rest and reflection." | 场景明确，选择理由具体 |
| 品牌信任 | "Every crystal should feel beautiful, but it should also come with clear sourcing, care guidance, and realistic expectations." | 同时表达审美、信任和边界 |

## 3. 反面语气案例

| 不要写成 | 问题 |
|----------|------|
| "This crystal will cure anxiety and remove all negative energy." | 医疗化、绝对化，容易造成信任和合规风险 |
| "Our crystals are the most powerful stones in the universe." | 空泛夸张，没有证据来源 |
| "Buy now before your energy collapses." | 恐吓式转化，不符合品牌温和可信的定位 |

## 4. 合规禁用词清单（GEO+FTC双重要求）

> **来源**：C端水晶站 GEO 优化策略 — 内容语气从"神秘功效"改成"象征意义 + 情绪价值 + 生活方式"（2026-06-01补充）
> **原则**：不宣称治疗疾病、保证结果或做医疗化声明。水晶 = 象征意义 + 情绪支持 + 生活方式饰品，不是医疗工具。
> **合规风险**：FTC（美国联邦贸易委员会）对健康声明的监管 + Google 对医疗错误信息的打击 + 社交平台对"虚假治愈"内容的审核。

### 4.1 禁用词 vs 替换词对照表

| ❌ 禁用词/句式 | ✅ 推荐替换 | 替换理由 |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| heal / cure / treat（疾病语境） | support / accompany / serve as a reminder | 避免医疗化声明 |
| "This crystal will cure anxiety" | "This crystal can support a sense of calm as part of your self-care routine" | 合规 + 情绪价值定位 |
| "attract wealth / bring money / guarantee fortune" | "symbolize abundance / represent prosperity intentions / many people carry as a wealth mindset reminder" | 不做绝对化承诺 |
| "change your destiny / alter your fate" | "mark meaningful life transitions / support personal growth intentions" | 避免占卜绝对化 |
| "scientifically proven to heal" | "traditionally associated with / many people report / used in spiritual practices for" | 不做虚假科学声明 |
| "100% guaranteed / absolutely will" | "known for / commonly used for / traditionally believed to" | 避免绝对化用语 |
| "replace medical treatment / alternative to therapy" | "complement your wellness routine / used alongside (not instead of) professional care" | 不暗示替代医疗 |
| "purify negative energy / remove all bad luck" | "support energetic balance / promote a sense of renewal / traditionally used for clearing" | 避免过度承诺 |
| "magic / supernatural power" | "meaningful ritual / symbolic intention / spiritual practice" | 品牌定位"灵性但不玄乎" |

### 4.2 安全表达框架

**三句话原则**：涉及功效时，标准表达结构为：

```
1. 传统/文化语境："Rose Quartz has been used for centuries as a symbol of unconditional love."
2. 个人体验/意图设定："Many people carry it as a gentle reminder to practice self-compassion."
3. 证据边界："While crystals cannot replace professional care, they can be a meaningful part of your mindfulness practice."
```

### 4.3 图片/社媒文案同样适用

禁用词清单不仅适用于网站文案和博客文章，同样适用于：
- Instagram/TikTok/Pinterest 图片文字叠加
- 产品描述和图片 alt 文本
- 邮件营销标题和正文
- FAQ 回答
- 用户评价展示（不得筛选/编辑出违规声明）

## 5. 销售页面禁用表达（Trust Bar / Comparison / Testimonials）

> **原则**：Earthward 不使用虚构数据、无法验证的绝对承诺、或能量治愈暗示。所有信任信号必须基于可验证的事实或合理的描述性声明。

### 5.1 禁止出现的表达

| ❌ 禁用表达 | 问题 | ✅ 替换 |
|-------------|------|---------|
| "4.9/5 Stars" / "10,000+ Happy Customers" | 虚构数据（新站无真实评价） | 描述性信任信号：Genuine Natural Crystals / Ethically Sourced / 30-Day Returns |
| "100% Natural" / "100% Guaranteed" | 绝对化承诺，FTC 风险 | "Genuine natural crystals" / "Hand-selected and verified authentic" |
| "Manifest abundance" | 能量治愈暗示 | "Set intentions around abundance and success" |
| "Shield your energy from negativity" | 能量保护暗示 | "Stay grounded with protective stones" |
| "Verified Buyer — [Name]" | 虚构评价（无真实交易） | 使用场景格式：For Calm Evenings / For Stressful Workdays |
| "Real Stories, Real Energy Shifts" | 暗示真实用户评价（实际是虚构的） | "How People Use Their Crystals" |
| "White sage smoke — rooted in Native American tradition" | 文化挪用风险 | "Smoke purification — a practice found in traditions across many cultures" |
| "Conflict-Free Guarantee" | 无法验证的绝对承诺 | "Responsible Sourcing" |
| "Purified with sage" | 暗示特定文化仪式 | "Energetically purified and charged under moonlight" |
| "Crystals That Work With Your Energy" | 能量治愈定位 | "Real Crystals for Intentional Living" |

### 5.2 信任信号规则

1. **不写虚构数字**：新站阶段不引用任何评价数量、客户数量、评分数据
2. **不写绝对承诺**：不用 "100%"、"guarantee"、"absolutely" 修饰产品属性
3. **不用虚构人名**：评价/故事区改为使用场景（For Calm Evenings / For Self-Compassion），不编造人名
4. **不用能量治愈语言**：不写 "work with your energy"、"shield your energy"、"energy shifts"
5. **不引用特定原住民仪式**：白鼠尾草改为泛文化描述 "smoke purification"

## 6. Brief 引用规则

- 默认语调：引用本文件，不需要每篇 Brief 重写正反案例。
- 特殊覆盖：爱情、财富、睡眠、风水、明星/流行文化等内容可以在 Brief 的"语气参考"中补充本篇特殊要求。
- 事实边界：涉及功效时优先使用 "traditionally used for"、"many people use"、"can serve as a reminder"，避免 "cure"、"guarantee"、"proven to heal"。
