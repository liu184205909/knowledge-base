- **合规措辞**：✅ traditionally associated with / often used as / many people choose it for / may support a sense of / can serve as a reminder to　❌ cures/treats/heals anxiety/removes trauma/guarantees protection/balances your energy instantly
- **FAQ scientifically proven**：每篇必留中性答

## 16. 生产强制规则（Color 独有风险防护）
- **石种重叠**（最大风险）：同一水晶在多颜色页出现（如 Amethyst 在 purple，Aquamarine 在 blue + blue-green）。**描述必须从"该颜色"视角重写，禁止复用单品 meaning 页**。例：Amethyst 在 purple-crystals 强调紫色成因（铁+辐照）+ 紫色象征（灵性/直觉/转化），非通用 meaning。此规则比 Chakra 版更刚性
- **Bathing 子方法**：必须标注「not all crystals are water-safe」（selenite/angelite 等怕水）
- **N 一致性**：标题去硬数字（用 `and Their Meanings`），或 N 严格跟随该色实际石种数
