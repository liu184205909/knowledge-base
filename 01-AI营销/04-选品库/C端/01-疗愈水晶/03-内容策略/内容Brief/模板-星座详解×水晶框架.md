## 二.五、内容生成规范（防 12 篇同质化）⭐ 2026-06-25 新增

> 12 篇如果只是替换变量（"Aries 换成 Cancer"），Google 会判 thin/duplicate content。以下是每个模块的**差异化写法要求**，agent 生成时必须遵守。

| 模块 | 每个星座必须差异化的内容 | 反面案例（禁止） |
|------|------------------------|----------------|
| **Basics** | Strengths/Challenges/Best Match 必须具体到行为 | ❌ "strong, loyal, passionate" |
| **Traits** | 至少 3 个**专属行为表现**（不是形容词） | ❌ "confident, energetic" → ✅ "fast to commit, hates waiting, drawn to challenge" |
| **Love** | 具体**恋爱行为**和关系模式 | ❌ "passionate in love" → ✅ "needs emotional safety, defensive shell, caretaker mode" |
| **Friendship** | 朋友关系里的**优点和摩擦点** | ❌ "a loyal friend" → ✅ "organizes every trip but resents when nobody helps" |
| **Career** | **适合行业、工作方式、金钱习惯** | ❌ "ambitious and hardworking" → ✅ "thrives in startups, hates red tape, saves for experiences" |
| **Challenges** | 绑定该星座**阴影面** | ❌ "can be stubborn" → ✅ Aries: "burns bridges before thinking" |
| **Crystals** | 对应**性格需求**选石 | ❌ "great for meditation" → ✅ "Carnelian for drive, Amethyst for the brake pedal" |
| **FAQ** | 至少 **2 个独有问题** | Aries: "Why so impulsive?" / Cancer: "Why does Cancer shut down?" |
