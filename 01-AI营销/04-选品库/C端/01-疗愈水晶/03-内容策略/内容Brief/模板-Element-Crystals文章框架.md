**Safety Note for Element Rituals**（M5 固定子段，Fire/Water 必有）：
> Use elemental practices symbolically and safely. Do not heat crystals, place them in flames, or soak water-sensitive stones. Some crystals fade in direct sunlight, and some should not be used in crystal-infused water (e.g., selenite, malachite, lapis). When in doubt, keep the crystal dry and use the element as a visual or meditative symbol.

## 8. M7 风水（改定位：风水实践应用，非五行对应）
- **定位转变**：不讲"{element} 对应五行哪一行"，讲"{element} 在风水实践中的应用"
- **显式声明**（每篇必备）：「The Western four-element system (earth, fire, air, water) and the Chinese five-element system (wood, fire, earth, metal, water) are different frameworks, not directly interchangeable.」
- **earth/fire/water**：讲相似点 + 差异点（五行有相生相克，四元素无）
- **air**（最危险，坦诚处理）：「Air is not one of the five classical Chinese elements. In Feng Shui, similar qualities (movement, breath, clarity, communication) may be discussed through qi, airflow, or Metal/Wood contexts — but Air should not be treated as a direct equivalent.」
- 方位用「often associated with」/「in many Feng Shui interpretations」（非绝对）
- 250-400 词，克制准确

## 9. ⚠️ Cross-System Accuracy Rule（强制，框架级锁死）
> **此规则最高优先级，违反即专业可信度受损。**
- 西方四元素（earth/fire/air/water）≠ 中国五行（木火土金水），**严禁画等号或混用相生相克**
- **air 篇不得强行塞入五行表**；用 qi/流动/通风方位类比
- **五行相生相克**（如需提及，必须人工校验）：生（木→火→土→金→水→木）；克（木→土→水→火→金→木）
- 西方星座与中国生肖**不可混用对应**
- M7 涉及方位/相生相克/生肖，**AI 生成后必须人工核对**，标记 `[需校验]` 待审
- 风水/命理内容加文化象征声明（非确定结果）
