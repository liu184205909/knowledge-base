## 6. M3 Numerology 段规范

**master number 严格定义**（数字学权威）：
- **Master numbers = 11, 22, 33**（仅此三个，不可扩展到 1111/2222/3333）
- 1111 = 1+1+1+1=4，或视为 11 的强化（但**不是标准 master number**）
- 文章中提及 master number **只在 11/22/33 页面使用**，其他号码不硬塞

**数字学拆解内容**（每篇必含）：
- 单数字能量（1=新开始/2=平衡/3=表达...）
- 重复放大（111=1 的能量×3 放大）
- 相加归约（666→6+6+6=18→1+8=9，关联"完成/智慧"）
- 该号码在 numerology 体系中的位置

## 13. Writing & Compliance Rules

**禁止表达**：
- ❌ "This number means you will become rich"
- ❌ "This crystal will attract your soulmate"
- ❌ "Seeing 111 guarantees manifestation"
- ❌ "This angel number is a message from angels"（作为绝对事实）
- ❌ "Use this crystal to heal anxiety/depression/illness"

**推荐表达**：
- ✅ "Many spiritual traditions interpret this number as..."
- ✅ "You may use this crystal as a mindfulness reminder for..."
- ✅ "This number can be seen as an invitation to reflect on..."
- ✅ "Crystals are not a substitute for medical, financial, or professional advice"

**三视角自然融入**（非独立标注,嵌入正文）：
> 示例："Spiritually, many people interpret {Number} as a sign of alignment. Psychologically, noticing repeated numbers can also work as a mindfulness cue, helping you pause and reflect on your current thoughts, choices, and emotions."

**Gentle Note**（全站统一组件,放 FAQ 前）：
> "Crystal and angel number meanings are based on spiritual traditions, symbolism, and personal mindfulness practices. They are not a substitute for medical, financial, or professional advice."

**去AI化**：同全站禁词 + 化学式大写 + **M6 水晶段 3-4 套句式轮换**（避免 500 个同结构句）
