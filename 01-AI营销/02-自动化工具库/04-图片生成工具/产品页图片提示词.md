# 产品页图片 AI 生成提示词

> **定位**：用于 AI 图片生成工具（Midjourney / DALL-E / Ideogram 等），生成电商产品页所需的一套完整图片。
> **适用场景**：Shopify / WordPress 产品页轮播图、落地页 Hero 图、广告素材
> **使用方式**：每个场景 prompt 末尾会自动附加「公共技术约束」，生成时将两者合并输入即可。

---

## 图片序列概览

产品页标准轮播图建议 6 张，覆盖完整转化路径。另附 D2C 广告素材场景用于付费社媒投放：

|------|------|------|----------|
| 1 | Hero 主图 | 第一印象，激发欲望 | 吸引停留 |
| 2 | 促销图 | 传达优惠/价值主张 | 推动行动 |
| 3 | 信任背书图 | 社会证明、客户评价 | 消除疑虑 |

| 7 | D2C 广告素材 | 付费社媒投放、落地页 Hero | 滚动停止、转化点击 |

## 场景 7：D2C 广告素材

> 适用于 Facebook/Instagram 广告、Shopify 落地页 Hero、付费社媒投放。生成纯产品图，文字/CTA 等设计元素在 Canva/Figma 中叠加。

```text
Create an ultra-premium D2C ecommerce advertisement hero image for this product.

Style reference: Grüns, AG1, Rhode, Huel, Apple, True Classic — bold, clean, conversion-focused, modern Shopify brand aesthetic.

The product is the hero. It must feel tactile, desirable, and instantly arresting in a social media feed.

Composition:
- Product centered and dominant
- Premium DTC brand feel
- Scroll-stopping visual impact
- Mobile-first composition
- Strong visual hierarchy

Background:
- Rich premium gradient OR soft diffused studio environment
- Dark luxury tones OR muted premium palette
- Subtle glow around product
- Creates contrast and depth
- Immersive, not flat

Lighting:
- Soft diffused cinematic lighting
- Luxury highlights and shadows
- Subtle rim glow
- Natural reflections
- Depth and realism

Product enhancement:
- Ultra sharp focus
- Material texture visible
- Premium finish
- High contrast
- Photorealistic detail

Mood:
- Bold, premium, aspirational
- Modern, healthy, trustworthy
- Visually addictive
- High-converting

Avoid:
- Flat plain backgrounds
- Corporate/generic stock look
- CGI-heavy rendering
- Over-saturation
- Cluttered props
- Multiple focal points

Output style: Ultra-premium DTC brand campaign image, 8K quality, scroll-stopping, conversion-focused.
```

> **使用说明**：此 prompt 生成**纯产品广告底图**。文字标题、benefit icons、CTA按钮、社交证明等设计元素，在 Canva/Figma 中用模板叠加。不要尝试让 AI 图片工具一次性生成包含文字的完整广告——文字渲染是 AI 图片工具的弱项。

## 公共技术约束

> 以下约束附加到每个场景 prompt 末尾，确保输出规格一致。

```text
Technical requirements:
- Generate the image in an exact 1:1 square aspect ratio
- Final image resolution must be exactly 2000x2000 pixels
- Design for product page image carousels (Shopify / WordPress / WooCommerce)
- Ensure all carousel images maintain identical dimensions and composition consistency
- Keep all important visual elements centered for mobile-responsive cropping
- Maintain a single clear focal point per image
- Ensure the product remains instantly recognizable at thumbnail size
- Optimize for mobile-first ecommerce viewing
- Prioritize fast visual comprehension and conversion clarity
- Use premium DTC ecommerce composition standards
- Avoid excessive empty space or uneven framing
- Maintain consistent lighting, framing quality, realism, and visual polish across the full carousel set
- Optimize for sharp zoom functionality on product pages
- Maintain photorealistic ultra high-definition quality
- Ensure the image feels consistent with premium modern ecommerce brands

Output style: Photorealistic, premium ecommerce campaign image, 8K quality, conversion-focused.
```

## 使用建议

1. **先生成 Hero 图**：确认产品风格和色调满意后，再依次生成其余 5 张，保持视觉一致性
2. **每次只生成一张**：不要尝试一次生成整套，逐张调整效果更可控
3. **产品名替换**：将 prompt 中的 "this product" 替换为具体产品名，如 "this amethyst crystal bracelet"
4. **适配 WordPress**：WordPress + WooCommerce 产品页同样适用，轮播图尺寸可根据主题调整为 800x800 或 1000x1000
5. **批量生产**：同一产品多个 SKU（如不同颜色水晶），保持 prompt 一致，只替换产品描述部分
